How to Use

The Click Queue

On the upper right corner, there is the click queue, which represents what has been clicked so far.
  • This symbol represents an empty spot, i.e. a location that doesn't have a node.
  • This symbol represents a node.
To clear the click queue, click Esc or hit the Esc key.

Adding Nodes

  1. Click anywhere that doesn't have a node.
  2. (Optional) Type a symbol for the label of the node. If no symbol is typed, an automatic counter is used as the label.
  3. Ensure the click queue is one empty slot. Click Enter or hit Enter/Return on the keyboard.

Adding Edges

  1. Click on two nodes.
  2. Ensure the click queue is two nodes. Click Enter or hit Enter/Return on the keyboard.
